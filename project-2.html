Project Overview

As part of a collaborative team, I spearheaded the development of a cutting-edge tool sharing app designed to foster community engagement among neighbors. Our platform enables seamless sharing of tools, empowering users to borrow and lend equipment for various projects. To enhance the user experience, we integrated an AI chatbot that intelligently assists in identifying the most suitable tools for specific tasks.

Key Achievements: App Architecture: Orchestrated the design and architecture of the tool sharing app, ensuring scalability, security, and ease of use. AI Integration: Used Google Gemmini API to integrate an AI chatbot. The chatbot provides personalized recommendations based on project requirements, user preferences, and historical usage patterns.
